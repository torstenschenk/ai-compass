### Persona 1: Martin Keller — CEO / Managing Director

Age: 49

Company: Family-owned manufacturing SME (~120 employees)

Job Title: Managing Director (Geschäftsführer)

**Daily Workflow**
Starts the day with emails and operational escalations
Short management meetings (sales, production, finance)
Jumps constantly between strategy and firefighting
Reads articles about AI “trends” late in the evening, half-skeptical

**Psychological Drivers**
Responsibility for the company’s long-term survival
Fear of missing out vs. fear of wasting money
Wants clarity, not technical detail

**Decision-Making Process**
Gut feeling first, validation second
Listens closely to trusted internal leads (IT, Ops)
Needs a clear story he can repeat to shareholders

**Biggest Frustrations**
Everyone talks about AI, nobody gives clear answers
Consultants feel expensive and abstract
Can’t tell if the company is behind or just cautious

**Secret Motivations**
- Wants to prove he’s a modern leader, not “old school”
- Afraid competitors are moving faster
- Wants something he can show in meetings, not just talk about

### Persona 2: Sabine Vogel — Head of IT

Age: 42
Company: Logistics SME (~300 employees)

Job Title: Head of IT

**Daily Workflow**
- Managing legacy systems and vendors
- Handling urgent tickets and infrastructure issues
- Occasional “Can we do AI?” questions from management
- Little time for strategic planning

**Psychological Drivers**
- Professional credibility
- Avoiding unrealistic expectations
- Desire to be taken seriously beyond “keeping the lights on”

**Decision-Making Process**
- Risk-aware and detail-oriented
- Prefers evidence over hype
- Needs to defend decisions upward and downward

**Biggest Frustrations**
- Management underestimates technical complexity
- AI expectations don’t match data reality
- No structured way to explain current limitations

**Secret Motivations**
- Wants AI to succeed—but on her terms
- Hopes for a neutral, external validation of her concerns
- Wants fewer “Why can’t we just use ChatGPT?” questions
